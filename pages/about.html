About me

My name is João Felipe Santos. I received my bachelor degree in Electrical Engineering from the Federal University of Santa Catarina (Brazil) in 2011 and my master’s and Ph.D in Telecommunications from the Institut National de la Recherche Scientifique (INRS) in 2013 and 2019. During my graduate studies, I was a member of MuSAE Lab at INRS and a visiting student at MILA at Université de Montréal. I am currently an AI Research Scientist at NVIDIA, where I work on all things conversational AI.
